About Me

Hello and welcome!

I'm a game designer/developer with a background in theatre and musical composition. I've been telling stories and building worlds through various mediums my entire life, and video game development has proven to be the perfect intersection of my artistic and technical passions. When COVID-19 hit in 2020, I decided it was finally time to build the programming skills I needed to bring my ideas to life.

Now, I'm excited to take my particular skillset, from deisigning a game, to building its code, to writing its narrative, to scoring its soundscape, and apply those in the context of a team of creators where I can learn from those with more experience and collaborate on projects far more ambitious than I could tackle on my own.

How I Work

It would be 100% fair to call me a perfectionist. I've gone on tangents with my siblings over a single word choice for a D&D campaign prologue, I've hashed out story arcs with writing partners over multiple late-night sessions, and I've spent longer than I care to admit fiddling with custom platforming physics.

And, dare I say, I would do it again, because I firmly believe that the little details matter. Maybe not every single person will notice, but I find so much joy and pride in crafting something to be just such a way, and even more joy in getting to have someone else experience that thing.

So if that sounds like what you're aiming for too, I'm down to help get us there.
